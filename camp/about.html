About Us

At CAMPTERRA, we offer more than just outdoor adventures—we create unforgettable experiences. Located in the heart of Sousse next to Sousse Med University, our camp is a place where kids, teens, and families can connect with nature, build lasting friendships, and challenge themselves through exciting activities. Our mission is simple: to inspire growth, foster teamwork, and create memories that last a lifetime. With experienced staff and a variety of fun activities, we provide a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment where everyone can feel at home. Join us at CAMPTERRA for endless adventures , laughter, and personal growth. We can’t wait to welcome you!
